Pope's Visitation to Ndorwa Prison

11 Aug 2016 by Admin   •   Comments 11

We were privileged to have the Pope visit us at our prison, this year and all in all it went very well . His Highness visit to Uganda, we were also privileged to have him in Kabaale District and he paid a visit to our prison in Ndorwa, he had brief talks and meeting with the prison officials and talk to our inmates.

The all prison was delighted to have his Highness come and pay a visit to us.

President's Visit to Ndorwa Prison

11 Aug 2016 by Admin   •   Comments 11

Am delighted to inform that we had the presidential visit last month and the all ceremony was marvellous for our all. The President visited us and he had a chance of looking and console all our inmates, some who were on bail, remand and others who had spent long hours in the prison.

Hopes are that he will still come back before the Christmas season
